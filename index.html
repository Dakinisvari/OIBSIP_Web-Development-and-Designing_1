About

Coastal Beacon helps tourists explore Tamil Nadu's beaches safely and smartly. It provides real-time weather and sea updates using live data, classifying beaches as Suitable, Caution, or Not Suitable for visits. You can view beach conditions on an interactive map, check past weather trends, and get email alerts during unsafe conditions. Tourists can also share photos, feedback, and ratings to help others plan better. The platform also promotes sustainable tourism by encouraging visitors to stay aware of environmental conditions and choose responsible travel practices.

Weather Trends & Safety Analytics

Coastal Beacon continuously monitors key parameters such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and pressure to evaluate current sea conditions. By analyzing three-day weather trends, our system intelligently classifies the beach's danger level — helping tourists plan safe and enjoyable coastal visits.

The platform transforms raw weather data into clear visual insights, ensuring you stay informed about the safety levels of your favorite beaches in real time.

Interactive Coastal Map

Explore Tamil Nadu’s coastline through our real-time interactive map. Each beach is informed with the weather conditions along with the top attractions, helping you make informed and confident travel decisions.
